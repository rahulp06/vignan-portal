About VGNT

Our Story

Vignan Institute of Technology and Science is the brainchild of Dr. L Rathaiah, Chairman, Vignan Group of Institutions, and was founded in 1999. VGNT is an exemplary institution of higher learning with a mission of pursuing excellence in education and research.

The institution, with its diverse and dynamic community of about 4000 students, offers a distinctive combination of accomplished faculty and world-class facilities, all set on a sprawling 350 acres of sylvan surroundings.

Our Core Values

Excellence and Innovation

We persevere in setting standards and attaining excellence by involving ourselves actively in the teaching and learning process.

Social Responsibility

Students are trained to be in cognizance of contemporary social issues and are encouraged to benefit the local community through associations like NSS.

Academic and Personal Integrity

We are committed to infusing in students strong Indian ethical values, integrity, respect, and commitment.
